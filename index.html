Our idea was to make gardening easier and more accessible to a greater number of people simply by expediting the gardening process. We have designed a composter that will take food scraps and other waste and turn them into compost in less time than normal.

To do this, we have designed a 3-compartment system: one where the raw waste is stored, one where the waste is converted into compost, and one to temporarily store the compost for later use.

Our product uses heat and motion to speed up the composting process in the second compartment. A timer allows for the compostin process to be fully automatedâ€”all the user has to do is take the ready made compost out!
